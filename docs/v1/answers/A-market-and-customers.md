## A. Market & Customer Questions

### Who exactly is our first 1000 customers:
- **Hard requirements**
    - Can afford to travel
    - Want to travel while they can, make the best of their youth
- **Desires**
    - Beginning to explore, stepping outside comfort zone
    - Want unique, creative ways to showcase their life
    - Want to capture hard-to-capture moments
    - Hold themselves to high standards, don't half-ass things
    - Want to look back with pride—evidence of life-defining moments
- **Outcomes**
    - Creative ways to document their lives (e.g., "wow I really want a camera for scuba diving")
- **Demographics**
    - Young adults, city folk with disposable income
    - Tech-savvy, comfortable with online platforms

### How often does an average customer rent per year?
- **Vacation renters**: 1-3 times per year
    - minimum once for annual vacation
    - could be multiple times for those who travel frequently
    - action needed: market research - to figure out frequency
- **Lifestyle documenters**: more frequent, potentially monthly
    - music producers capturing live band experiences for marketing
    - content creators documenting music videos or creative projects
    - hobbyists pursuing regular outdoor activities (diving, hiking, etc.)
    - what is the frequency beyond which its more worth it for someone to buy it instead of renting from us
    - deals with other retailers if we refer them customers who rent with us to the point we want to buy
    - deals between customers who refer each other also (more on marketing now)
    - deal with them, if they rent enough with us we can just give them the thing at a discount (loyalty) - need to think more about this
    - friction point - renting to someone too regular could not be realistic, they might as well buy their own
- **Key insight**: Not just vacation-focused - also for documenting life in general

### Is this a "once-a-year vacation" product or a lifestyle product?
- **Both**, depending on customer segment:
    - Casual adventurers: once-a-year vacation product
    - Content creators & hobbyists: lifestyle product with regular usage
- The flexibility of our model supports both use cases

### What percentage of your customers are:
- **Primary focus**: Casual adventurers (50-60%)
    - Annual or bi-annual travelers
    - Want professional documentation without commitment
- **Secondary focus**: Content creators / lifestyle (25-35%)
    - Musicians, artists, small business owners
    - Need equipment for marketing and brand building
- **Tertiary**: Serious hobbyists (10-20%)
    - Regular outdoor enthusiasts
    - May eventually buy, but want to trial first

### Why would someone choose renting over buying long-term?
- **Freedom of choice**: Try different equipment every time, no commitment to one model
- **Financial flexibility**: No large upfront investment (action cameras can cost RM2000-5000+)
- **Chase latest tech**: Always access newest models without buying and reselling
- **Trial before purchase**: Test equipment they're considering buying
- **Episodic usage**: Only need gear occasionally, not worth owning
- **No maintenance burden**: No storage, cleaning, repairs, or upgrades to worry about - note from nadree (we should be more educated about insurance, maintenance that we can do ourselves)
- **Activity-specific needs**: Different activities need different gear configurations
