# Business Cheat Sheet

## 🎯 WHAT WE ARE

**Adventure gear rental service** that becomes an **identity and lifestyle brand** for young Malaysian adventurers.

**Core Mission:** Enable people to document adventurous, meaningful life experiences with zero friction.

**Tagline Philosophy:** "If not now, when?"

---

## 👥 WHO WE SERVE

**Primary (50-60%):** Casual adventurers - 1-3 trips/year, want professional documentation  
**Secondary (25-35%):** Content creators/lifestyle - musicians, artists, regular documenters  
**Tertiary (10-20%):** Serious hobbyists - trial before buying, regular outdoor enthusiasts

**Demographics:** Young adults (mid-20s to early 30s), Klang Valley, disposable income, tech-savvy

---

## 💡 CORE VALUE PROPOSITIONS

1. **Activity-ready kits** - not just cameras, complete solutions for diving, hiking, etc.
2. **Flexible pickup/dropoff** - separate from rental period, time to transfer footage
3. **Wear-and-tear tolerance** - adventure-aware, no penalties for reasonable use
4. **Seamless digital experience** - real-time availability, transparent pricing, customer accounts
5. **Low barrier to entry** - reasonable deposits via insurance, minimal legal friction
6. **Community & identity** - not just rental, a brand adventurers vibe with

---

## 🔥 DIFFERENTIATION (OUR MOAT)

1. **Bold identity** - resonates with young Klang Valley adventurers
2. **Seamless UX** - self-service website, convenient logistics
3. **Community focus** - consultation-based service, events (like Table & Apron)
4. **Insurance structure** - enables low deposits, adventure-friendly policies
5. **Premium but accessible** - trusted quality, raw/adventurous branding (not luxury)

**Why us over competitors:** Identity + lowest friction + we care more about their experience

---

## 💰 UNIT ECONOMICS (ROUGH)

- **Action cam cost:** RM 2,000-3,000
- **Rental rate:** RM 60-80/day (premium models)
- **Break-even:** ~50 rental days per item
- **Lifespan consideration:** New models yearly, rental prices drop annually
- **Deposit strategy:** Lower deposits (insurance-backed) vs. traditional 50% deposits

---

## 🎨 BRAND POSITIONING

**Not:** Luxury premium OR hardcore-only OR neighborhood shop  
**Yes:** Trusted, raw, adventurous, accessible to all skill levels

**Dual messaging:**
- **To adventurers:** Up your creativity game
- **To lifestyle creators:** Step out of comfort zone, experiment more

**Core values:** Novelty, growth, challenging yourself, never settling for comfort

**Inspiration sources:** Red Bull (passion for adventure), Secret Life of Walter Mitty

---

## 🚀 WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT FROM CURRENT MARKET

**Current rental pain points we solve:**
- ❌ WhatsApp catalogs → ✅ Real-time online availability
- ❌ Manual back-and-forth → ✅ Transparent self-service booking
- ❌ Google Forms every time → ✅ Customer accounts with saved info
- ❌ QR code transfers → ✅ Professional payment system
- ❌ Intimidating legal docs → ✅ Streamlined, insurance-backed process
- ❌ 50% deposits → ✅ Reasonable deposits
- ❌ Pickup only during rental → ✅ Flexible pickup/dropoff windows
- ❌ Just equipment → ✅ Complete activity kits

---

## 📊 CUSTOMER PHILOSOPHY

**Why rent vs buy:**
- Freedom to try different equipment
- No RM 2-5k upfront investment
- Always access latest tech
- Trial before purchase
- Episodic usage (not worth owning)
- Zero maintenance burden

**Rental frequency:**
- Vacation: 1-3x/year
- Lifestyle: Monthly potential
- Need to balance: too frequent = should buy instead (offer loyalty deals/referrals)

---

## 🛡️ RISK & TRUST STRATEGY

**Worst case:** Customer disappears with gear + full kit  
**Mitigation:**
- Insurance-backed model (lower deposits, better experience)
- Documentation: photos before/after every rental
- NRIC/eKYC for identity (exploring alternatives to NRIC copies)
- Legal recourse as scare tactic (may not pursue every case)

**Dispute handling:** Evidence-based (photos), mediation-first, maintain trust

---

## 🔧 OPERATIONS ESSENTIALS

**Inventory:** Build custom tracking system, link to storefront, real-time availability  
**Inspection:** Document before/after, define "acceptable wear" standards  
**Delivery:** Optional top-up fee (Shopee-like experience) vs. physical collection  
**Turnaround:** Buffer time between rentals for inspection/maintenance  
**Peak seasons:** Vacation-focused = seasonal; lifestyle-focused = year-round stability

---

## 📈 GROWTH STRATEGY

**Phase 1:** Klang Valley, action cameras + adventure kits  
**Phase 2:** Expand gear categories (lifestyle/creative equipment)  
**Phase 3:** Become the top rental service in KL/PJ

**Community building:**
- Events and meetups
- Consultation-based service to help customers find the best combo for their vacation
- Content that inspires (not just sells)
- Partnerships: dive shops, tour operators, gyms
