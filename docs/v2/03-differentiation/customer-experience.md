# Customer Experience

## Experience Philosophy

### Priority: Convenience First, Relationships When Feasible

**Not Either/Or, But Sequential:**
1. Build scalable, seamless operations foundation
2. Layer in personal relationships where feasible
3. Consultation is tool for relationships, not business model itself

**Why This Order:**
- Seamless operations enable scale
- Personal touch differentiates but doesn't block growth
- Both are important, but convenience unlocks the business

### 5. Pickup / Delivery

---

#### Option B: Physical Collection

**Experience:**
- Agreed pickup location (convenient, safe, public)
- Go through gear usage with customer
- Answer questions, provide tips
- Build personal relationship
- Immediate troubleshooting

**Experience Goal:**
- Welcomed and supported
- Confident they have everything
- Personal connection established
- Excited and prepared

---

### 6. During Rental Period

**Touchpoints:**
- Equipment in use

- Wear-and-tear tolerance (no anxiety about damage)

**Experience Goal:**
- Empowered to capture amazing moments
- No fear of damage (reasonable wear accepted)
- Proud of what they're creating
- Support available if issues arise

**Key Differentiator:**
- Wear-and-tear tolerance explicitly communicated
- "Use the gear fully, don't hold back"
- We understand adventurers push equipment
- No penalties for reasonable use

---

### 7. Return / Dropoff

**Touchpoints:**

**Experience Goal:**

**Process:**

- Quick inspection with customer present (if physical) or documented (if delivery)
- Compare to outgoing condition
- Assess against "acceptable wear" standards
- Deposit returned promptly if no issues

## Service Quality Standards

### Not Mechanical Like McDonald's

**What We Avoid:**
- Kiosk-style self-service only
- No human interaction
- Transactional and forgettable
- One-size-fits-all approach

**What We Do Instead:**
- Authentic like cafes with passion (Table & Apron, Bijou Cafe)
- Personal touch when feasible
- Care more than customers do about their experience
- Flexible and human

---

### Authentic Customer Relationships

**Goal:** Customers leave thinking "wow, the people are so friendly, I want to keep renting from them."

**How:**
- Genuine care (can't be faked)
- Proactive support (anticipate needs)
- Friendly and approachable (not corporate)
- Remember repeat customers
- Go extra mile when possible

**Balance:**
- Scalable systems (don't block growth)
- Personal touch (differentiate experience)
- Efficient but not rushed
- Professional but not cold

---

## Support & Consultation

### For First-Timers

**Approach:**
- Option to ask questions upfront and center (not buried)
- Effortless support (low friction to get help)
- Consultation available if needed
- Proactive suggestions based on their needs

**Examples:**
- "First time diving? Here's what we recommend..."
- "Not sure what you need? Let's chat and figure it out"
- "We care as much as you do about your trip — maybe more"

**Not:**
- Consultation as core business model (not required)
- Forced interaction (some want self-service)
- Time-consuming process (efficient but helpful)

---

### For Experienced Users

**Approach:**
- Self-service works great (they know what they want)
- Support available if needed
- Customization options (build your own kit)
- Peer-to-peer tone (not teaching, collaborating)

**Examples:**
- "Need something specific? Customize your kit"
- "Pro tip: this mount works great for..."
- "Let us know if you need anything else"

---

## Handling Different Customer Segments

### Casual Adventurers (50-60%)

**Needs:**
- Simple, clear options
- Confidence they have everything
- No anxiety about damage
- Easy process

**Experience:**
- Pre-configured kits (no decision paralysis)
- Clear instructions and support
- Wear-and-tear tolerance communicated
- Friendly, encouraging tone

---

### Content Creators / Lifestyle (25-35%)

**Needs:**
- Variety of equipment
- Flexibility and customization
- ROI-focused (equipment enables revenue)
- Reliable and professional

**Experience:**
- Customizable kits
- Quick turnaround for frequent rentals
- Account history and preferences saved
- Peer-to-peer relationship

---

### Serious Hobbyists (10-20%)

**Needs:**
- Specific equipment (trial before purchase)
- Latest tech access
- Knowledgeable staff
- Respect for expertise

**Experience:**
- Full customization options
- Detailed equipment specs
- Loyalty program (path to purchase)
- Expert-to-expert conversation

---

## Dispute Resolution & Difficult Situations

### Philosophy: Maintain Trust Even in Disagreement

**Approach:**
1. Evidence-based (photos/videos from inspection)
2. Compare to "acceptable wear" standards
3. Mediation and compromise first
4. When to absorb costs vs. charge customers
5. Maintain relationship even if charging

**Communication:**
- Empathetic and understanding
- Clear explanation with evidence
- Fair and consistent application
- Open to discussion
- Focus on resolution, not blame

**Goal:** Customer may not be happy about charge, but respects fairness and maintains relationship.
