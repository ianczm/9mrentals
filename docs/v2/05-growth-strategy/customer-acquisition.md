# Customer Acquisition Strategy

## Content Strategy: Red Bull-Inspired Marketing

### Core Concept

**UGC of people pushing themselves to the limit** — "this is what we stand for"

**Not:**
- ❌ Boring testimonials ("great service, 5 stars")
- ❌ Product-focused content (features and specs)
- ❌ Polished, fake perfection
- ❌ Just adventure sports

**Yes:**
- ✅ People pushing limits (adventure + life moments)
- ✅ First steps outside comfort zone
- ✅ Life-defining experiences worth remembering
- ✅ Authentic stories under our brand

---

### Content Examples

**Adventure Moments:**
- First dive, first mountain climb
- Solo travel to challenging destination
- Pushing physical limits
- Overcoming fear of heights, water, etc.

**Life Moments:**
- First child (documenting parenthood journey)
- Confessing to crush (taking emotional risk)
- Quitting job to travel (life-changing decision)
- Any moment that makes life beautiful and worth remembering

**Key:** Not just adventure — any first step outside comfort zone that defines life.

---

### Content Production

**Sources:**
- Customer-generated content (UGC)
- Founder-created content (early phase)
- Community contributions
- Partnerships with adventurers and creators

**Format:**
- Short-form video (Instagram Reels, TikTok)
- Photo stories with captions
- Long-form video (YouTube) for deeper stories
- Written stories (blog, social media)

**Production Quality:**
- High production value (Red Bull-style)
- Professional editing and storytelling
- Authentic moments, not staged
- Emotional resonance, not just visuals

**Frequency:**
- Consistent posting schedule
- Quality over quantity
- Build content library over time
- Seasonal and timely content

---

### Customized Branding Campaigns

**Goal:** Stand out like Red Bull does with unique, memorable campaigns

**Campaign Ideas:**

1. **"If Not Now, When?" Campaign**
   - Push people to take the leap
   - Feature people who took action
   - Before/after transformation stories
   - Call-to-action: What are you waiting for?

2. **"Moments Worth Remembering" Series**
   - UGC showcasing limit-pushing experiences
   - Diverse range of moments (not just extreme sports)
   - Emotional storytelling
   - Community-driven content

3. **"First Times" Campaign**
   - Celebrate all kinds of first steps
   - First dive, first solo trip, first time saying "I love you"
   - Vulnerability and courage
   - Relatable and inspiring

4. **"Local Heroes" Positioning**
   - Malaysian adventurers and their stories
   - "We do what others only talk about"
   - Localized content, authentic to Malaysia
   - Community spotlight

**Execution:**
- Launch campaigns seasonally or around events
- Integrate across all channels
- Encourage community participation
- Measure engagement and impact

## Referral & Loyalty Programs

### Referral Program

**Structure:**
- Refer a friend, both get discount
- Tiered rewards (more referrals = bigger rewards)
- Easy sharing (unique referral link)
- Track and reward automatically

**Benefits:**
- Low-cost customer acquisition
- High-quality leads (friend referrals)
- Network effects
- Community building

---

### Loyalty Program

**Structure:**
- Points per rental
- Tiered benefits (bronze, silver, gold)
- Exclusive perks (early access, priority booking)
- Path to purchase (rent enough, get discount to buy)

**Benefits:**
- Increase repeat rental rate
- Higher customer lifetime value
- Competitive moat
- Customer data and insights

---

### Retailer Partnership Program

**Structure:**
- Refer customers who want to buy
- Earn affiliate fees from retailers
- Customer gets discount for being loyal renter
- Win-win-win (customer, us, retailer)

**Benefits:**
- Additional revenue stream
- Maintains relationship even after they stop renting
- Helps customers at threshold where buying makes sense
- Partnerships with gear retailers

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Targets

### Need to Define:

**Acceptable CAC:**
- Based on customer lifetime value (CLV)
- Target: CAC < 1/3 of CLV (payback in first rental)
- Varies by channel and segment

**Channel-Specific CAC:**
- Organic social media: Low (time investment)
- Paid advertising: Medium-High (budget dependent)
- Referrals: Very low (incentive cost only)
- Partnerships: Low-Medium (commission or revenue share)
- SEO: Low long-term (upfront investment)

**Tracking:**
- CAC by channel
- CAC by customer segment
- Payback period
- ROI per channel
- Optimize based on data
