## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Research Invalidates Assumptions

**Mitigation:** Better to learn early than after investment. Pivot or adjust based on findings.

### Risk: Technology Development Delays

**Mitigation:** Hire developer if needed, use no-code tools initially, or simplify MVP.

### Risk: Inventory Delays

**Mitigation:** Order early, have backup suppliers, start with fewer kits if needed.

### Risk: Pilot Reveals Major Issues

**Mitigation:** That's the point of pilot. Refine and test again before wider launch.

### Risk: Running Out of Time or Money

**Mitigation:** Prioritize ruthlessly, cut scope if needed, secure sufficient capital upfront.
