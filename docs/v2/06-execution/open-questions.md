# Open Questions & Research Needed

## Critical Questions (Must Answer Before Launch)

### 1. Insurance Viability

**Questions:**
- What does rental equipment insurance cost in Malaysia?
- What does insurance actually cover (theft, damage, wear)?
- At what scale does insurance become economical?
- What are exclusions and limitations?
- What's the claim process and deductibles?

**Why Critical:** Core to our low-deposit strategy and competitive advantage.

**Action:** Get quotes from Malaysian insurers, understand coverage details, calculate break-even.

---

### 2. "Acceptable Wear" Definition

**Questions:**
- What specific criteria define acceptable vs. unacceptable wear?
- How do we create visual standards that are consistent?
- What's acceptable for different equipment types?
- How do we train inspectors for consistency?

**Why Critical:** Core promise to customers, risk of disputes if unclear.

**Action:** Create visual guide with photo examples, develop inspection checklist, test with pilot customers.

---

### 3. Customer Verification Method

**Status: Partially resolved.** MVP uses a custom onboarding survey (customer profile, risk/reliability, marketing fit). eKYC is deferred to post-MVP.

**Remaining post-MVP questions:**
- Which eKYC providers are available in Malaysia?
- What's the cost and UX quality of each?
- What's legally enforceable vs. a custom survey?
- At what point does the investment in eKYC make sense?

**Why Still Relevant:** When we're ready to enhance verification beyond the custom survey, these questions need answers.

**Action:** Revisit post-MVP when operationally ready.

---

### 4. Pricing Strategy

**Questions:**
- What's the optimal rental rate (daily, weekly, monthly)?
- How much should delivery cost?
- What deposit amount is acceptable to customers?
- How to price customization vs. pre-configured kits?
- Peak season pricing strategy?

**Why Critical:** Affects profitability, competitiveness, and customer perception.

**Action:** Competitor analysis, customer interviews on willingness to pay, test pricing with early customers.
