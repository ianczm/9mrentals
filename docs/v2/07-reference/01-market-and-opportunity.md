### Key Gaps in Current Market

| Gap | Detail |
|---|---|
| No modern digital UX | No real-time availability, no instant booking, no customer accounts |
| No activity-ready kits | Competitors rent individual items — customers must figure out accessories themselves |
| No flexible logistics | Rental period = pickup/dropoff period, no separation, limited delivery |
| No brand identity | All competitors are purely transactional, no community or storytelling |
| High friction & anxiety | 50%+ deposits, unclear damage policies, fear of breaking gear |
| Poor customer experience | WhatsApp chaos, no self-service, limited hours, no post-rental engagement |

`[CORE]` Each gap maps directly to a differentiator in our model.

### Risks to Monitor

| Risk | Detail | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Low market awareness | Many potential customers don't know camera rentals exist | SEO, social media, adventure community partnerships |
| Seasonal demand | Peak during school holidays, low off-peak | Lifestyle segment (content creators) for year-round revenue |
| Competitor response | Existing players may copy digital approach | Build brand moat early — community, trust, relationships |
| Equipment damage/loss | Adventure gear faces high wear-and-tear | Clear damage policy, insurance (Phase 2), eKYC, deposits |
| Low inventory utilization | Poor unit economics if gear sits idle | Start with 3–5 kits, scale on demand, multi-day discounts |
