## Core Philosophy

**Tagline: "If not now, when?"**

- Pull the trigger to step into a new chapter of life
- Do something you're afraid of
- Stop finding 1,000 ways to avoid what you want
- If there's a will, there's a way — isolate signal from noise
- Applies beyond adventure: any first step outside comfort zone

`[CORE]` This is not just a tagline — it's the filter for every brand, content, and product decision.

## Content Strategy

`[RESOLVED]` Content is a **marketing tool**, not a revenue-generating product.

### Approach: Red Bull-Inspired UGC

**Yes:**
- UGC of people pushing themselves to the limit
- First steps outside comfort zone (not just extreme sports)
- Life-defining experiences worth remembering
- Authentic stories under our brand
- Behind-the-scenes — Gen Z values authenticity over polish

**Not:**
- Boring testimonials
- Product-focused content
- Polished, fake perfection
- Just adventure sports

### Content Categories (Priority Order)

| Priority | Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Short, informative, behind-the-scenes | Practical, builds trust, shows authenticity |
| 2 | User-generated content | Personality, branding, impact, testimonials |
| 3 | Structured tutorials | How to use and care for equipment |

*(Source: minutes-27022026)*

### Types of Stories to Tell

- First dive, first mountain climb, solo travel to challenging destination
- First child, confessing to a crush, quitting job to travel
- Any moment that makes life beautiful and worth remembering
- **Not just adventure** — any life-defining first step

## Key Lessons (Consolidated)

1. Brand is bigger than product — stand for something beyond the transaction
2. Passion is contagious — care more than customers expect
3. Community is the moat — relationships and identity are hard to copy
4. Action over words — do things others only talk about
5. Authentic stories inspire — real moments, not polished perfection
6. Adventure is about feeling alive — not just adrenaline, but meaning
7. Technology enables trust — but human touch still matters
8. Quality execution matters — people remember how you made them feel

---

## Decision Filter

When making brand, content, or product decisions, ask:

- Does this align with "if not now, when?"
- Does this help people push outside comfort zones?
- Does this build community and identity?
- Does this inspire action, not just sell product?
- Does this show we care more than expected?
- Is this authentic and real, not fake or polished?
