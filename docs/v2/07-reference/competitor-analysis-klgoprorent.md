Competitive Analysis Deep-Dive

KLGoProRent - No. 1 in KL and Selangor

---

## Pros

**Very warm and friendly service**

They’re just as passionate about our travels as us

**Hidden loyalty discount**

If they like you, they’ll let you know

**Usually provides a standard set of equipment without add-on prices**

Dive case, shoulder strap, extra batteries, etc.

**Charge does not include pick-up day**

Customers don’t pay for days they don’t use the cam

**Very good track record and online presence - 1 decade of rentals**

Good reputation and trustworthiness - peace of mind

**Has Consoles and Drones aswell.**

Different activities available to broaden their demographic

## Typical Customer Experience

**Find contact on Instagram**

[Good] They do some promotions + a lot of word of mouth and testimonials which is reassuring

- Insta

**Message them on WhatsApp if interested**

[Good] They have a business catalog where you can make orders

- WhatsApp

**Get a quotation from them via WhatsApp, valid for a day**

[Bad] Pricing changes by demand / season, not transparent

- WhatsApp

**Fill out the booking form**

[Typical] Personal details collected e.g. NRIC, address, travel location, pick-up arrangement, etc.

- Google Forms

**Make instant transfer to their bank account**

[Bad] There’s no automated checkout, just “transfer to this account” - cards not accepted
(But since Jun 2025, they accept online payments via card)

- Online Banking

**Forward receipt via WhatsApp and wait for confirmation**

- WhatsApp

**Once payment confirmed, arrange exact pick-up time and location**

[Bad] Sometimes another contact will be shared for the pick-up

- WhatsApp

**Drive to their pick-up location and exchange goods**

[Good] They will demo live how to use the cam, very helpful
(They also take a picture of you for marketing purposes)

- Pick-Up Day - F2F

**Go on the trip**

[Good] They’re very responsive on WhatsApp

- Trip

**Drive to their drop-off location**

[Good] They’ll perform a quick check of everything <5 mins
[Bad] Sometimes far

- Drop-Off Day - F2F
